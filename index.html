About Us

Royals of Troy isn’t just another spot to grab a bite — it’s a local, family-owned restaurant built on pride, care, and a love for great food. Every cheesesteak, burger, and sandwich is made the way we’d serve it to our own family — fresh, hearty, and full of flavor. As a family-run business, we’re not just serving meals — we’re building connections with our community. From our kitchen to your table, our goal is simple: to treat every customer like royalty and make sure you leave full, happy, and ready to come back for more.
